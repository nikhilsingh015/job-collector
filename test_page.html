Software Technical Team Lead


About the Role


At T-Pro our vision is simple! Be the leading AI-powered clinical documentation and workflow platform, allowing health care professionals to spend more time with patients, doing the work that matters.

We are seeking an experienced Technical Team Lead to lead and grow one of our teams. This is an excellent opportunity for a technically proficient individual who thrives in collaborative environments and is passionate about building scalable, reliable software systems.

As an Team Lead, you will guide and mentor a team of approximately 7 engineers, comprising Software Engineers, QA Engineers, and QA Automation Engineers. You will be hands-on in technical decision-making while fostering a culture of continuous improvement, collaboration, and engineering excellence.


At T-Pro, we believe that great software is built by diverse teams. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where your voice is heard, and your contributions make a real difference in the lives of healthcare professionals and patients.


Key Responsibilities


People Leadership

  • Lead, coach, and develop a team of ~7 engineers across software development and quality assurance.
  • Conduct regular 1:1s, performance reviews, and career development conversations.
  • Foster an inclusive, psychologically safe team environment where all members can contribute and grow.
  • Identify training needs and support professional development opportunities.
  • Participate in recruitment, interviewing, and onboarding of new team members.

Agile Delivery & Process

  • Ensure Scrum ceremonies are effective and valuable.
  • Collaborate with Product Owners to refine backlogs, prioritize work, and plan sprints.
  • Remove impediments and enable the team to deliver consistently and predictably.
  • Track team velocity, quality metrics, and delivery KPIs to drive continuous improvement and meet our OKR targets.
  • Champion engineering best practices including code reviews, testing, responsible AI adoption and documentation

Technical Leadership

  • Provide technical guidance on backend architecture, system design, and implementation strategies.
  • Collaborate with architects and senior engineers on technical decisions and roadmaps.
  • Contribute to technical strategy and help evolve our platform architecture.


Technical Environment

Our platform comprises multiple interconnected systems:

  • Backend Services: NestJS/Node.js, TypeScript, Fastify, PostgreSQL, Redis
  • AI/ML Integration: Python, FastAPI, OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, Google Cloud Speech
  • Real-time Communication: Socket.IO, WebSockets, Twilio Voice/Video
  • Search & Analytics: OpenSearch/Elasticsearch
  • Infrastructure: Docker, Kubernetes (AWS EKS), AWS (S3, Lambda, ECR)
  • Monitoring: Sentry, Prometheus, Grafana
  • Frontend (context): Angular 18, RxJS, Angular Material


About You

We are looking for someone who brings a mix of technical expertise and thoughtful leadership to our engineering team. You do not need to check every single box to apply.


Important to have

  • You have roughly 7+ years of experience in backend development at a senior level with at least 3 years of that managing people.
  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, Software Engineering, or a related technical field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • Demonstrated experience leading or mentoring engineering teams.
  • Solid understanding of Agile/Scrum methodologies.
  • Proficiency in Node.js/TypeScript or similar backend technologies.
  • Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred), caching systems (Redis), and queue-based architectures.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and a track record of delivering high-quality software.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to collaborate across teams.

Nice to Have

  • Experience with OKRs and managing team objectives.
  • Experience with QA automation frameworks and testing strategies.
  • Practical experience with AI/LLM services, agent-based systems, RAG system, etc.
  • Familiarity with healthcare technology, clinical systems or regulated environments.


Why Join Us?

  • Meaningful Work: Your code directly supports doctors and improves patient outcomes.
  • Flexibility: We support hybrid work arrangements that fit your life.
  • Growth: We invest in your learning and development.
  • Inclusive Culture: We are actively building a team that represents a variety of backgrounds, perspectives, and skills.
